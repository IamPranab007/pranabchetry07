About The School

GYANAM ACADEMY SENIOR SECONDARY SCHOOL BORGANG, BISWANATH, ASSAM Gyanam Academy Senior Secondary School, Borgang, Behali, Biswanath, Assam is an Institution under the management of 'Biswanath Gyanam Educational And Welfare Society', a Registered Society under the Societies Registration Act XXI, 1860. The School is affiliated to 'Assam Higher Secondary Education Council, Guwahati, Assam. The school was established in the year 2012.
