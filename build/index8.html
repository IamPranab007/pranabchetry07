Rules Of Examinations And Promotions

1. The students who absent themselves from the examinations will in no case be re-examined.

2. Absense from one or more subject result in the loss of marks for the subjects and excludes that pupil from being reckoned in the order of merit or rank in the examination

3. There will be unique test which will be stretched over the year

4. No examination will taken either before or after scheduled time
