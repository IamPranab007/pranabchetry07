Our Features And Facilities

Science Lab

Gyanam provides a science lab for conducting scientific researches. It helps students to remember the concepts better and to transfer the experience to real-life situations. It is a facility that provides controlled conditions in which scientific research, experiments and measurements may be performed.







Computer Lab

The computer lab serves as a place where students can come to learn different technological applications, such as virtual design or even excel spreadsheets. It can also serve as a place for students who don't have personal devices to do homework, research, or work on other online assignments.







Excursion

The excursion was not just about learning; it also provided an opportunity for bonding and team building. It gives students the chance to experience new venues. School field trips build in children a different level of excitement and fun. It gives them something to look forward to. Educational Excursions enhance the students' knowledge and broaden the outlook towards the world beyond classroom training.











Video Class

Gyanam provides video class in school where students primarily access educational content through videos, often incorporating elements like lectures, demonstrations, animations, allowing for a visually engaging and interactive way to learn particulary beneficial for visual learners and diverse learning styles.







Activity Based Class

Gyanam provides activity based class is, most simply, creating tasks, situations, activities, and other instructional ways to guide children to acquire, understand, and apply new information and skills that will help them in their studies and their lives.







Hostel Facility

Each student is provided with a study table, chair and has 24-hour electricity and water supply, water cooler, common telephone, and common TV room.







Bus Facility

Gyanam provides bus facility which is a vital service that offer to ensure that students have a safe and reliable way to commute to and from school. This service not only helps alleviate the burden of transportation for parents but also ensures that students can arrive at school on time and safely return home afterward
