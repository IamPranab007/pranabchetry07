Rules For The Payment of Fees

1. The fees are charged throughout the year and it has to be paid as long as the pupil's name is on rolls.

2.The fee is being collected through offline or online mode.

3. Parents will kindly keep the receipts, issued to them and produce them in proof of payment if needed.

4.Money once remitted will not be refunded.

5. A late fee of Rs. 10/per day will be charged after the 10th of the second month and Rs. 30/ after the third month.

6.Any student who is issued Transfer Certificate from the school has to pay the whole amount of the installment of the month included.
